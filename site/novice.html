Adjusted competitive pace

Was the front of the Novice B field faster or slower than other years?

The headline measure is the median of the top three boats—the second-place time—adjusted by how fast or slow a broad basket of other events ran that same day. An index above 100 is faster than the typical season in the same distance era. Men’s 2023 half-mile results are kept separate from the quarter-mile years.
